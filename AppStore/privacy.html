Privacy Policy

Sana — AI Nutrition Coach  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Sana ("the app") is a personal nutrition tracking application developed by Çağrı Demir. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data Sana collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device never sent to us

Sana stores all personal data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Meal entries — name, calories, macros, health score, photos, timestamps
  • Weight entries — dates and weights
  • Water intake entries — amounts and timestamps
  • Supplement logs — supplement names, dosages, check-in history
  • User profile — name, age, height, weight, goals, dietary preferences, health conditions
  • Meal plans — AI-generated weekly plans and grocery lists
  • Coach conversations — chat history with the AI coach
  • Progress photos — body progress images stored in your device's private container

This data is stored in your device's private container and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account.

Data sent off-device

Sana sends certain data to third-party services only to provide app functionality:

Anthropic Claude API: Meal images and text are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to provide real-time nutrition analysis and coaching. Data sent to the API is not used to train Anthropic's models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Firebase: Crash reports contain no personally identifiable information. Analytics events are anonymised and aggregated.

Apple Health

Sana may request access to Apple HealthKit to read:

  • Step count and active energy burned
  • Sleep analysis
  • Resting heart rate

This data is read from your Health app and displayed within Sana. It is never sent to any external server. Access is optional and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health → Sana.

Authentication

Sana supports Sign in with Apple, Sign in with Google, and email/password sign-in via Firebase Authentication. We receive only the minimum information required to create your account (a unique identifier and, optionally, your name and email). We do not store your credentials — sign-in state is managed securely by Apple/Google/Firebase.

Progress Photos

Progress photos are stored exclusively on your device's private app container. They are never uploaded, backed up to our servers, or shared with any third party. If you enable iCloud backup, photos may be included in your personal iCloud backup under your own Apple ID.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is shared only as described above, solely to provide and improve app functionality.

Data Retention

  • Device data: Stored until you delete the app or clear app data.
  • iCloud data: Stored in your personal iCloud account under your control.
  • API data: Messages sent to the Claude API are processed in real time per Anthropic's data retention policy.
  • Firebase data: Crash reports are retained for 90 days. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your meal data via Profile → Export meal data (CSV)
  • Delete all local data by deleting the app
  • Revoke HealthKit access in iOS Settings at any time
  • Request data deletion by contacting us at the email below

Children

Sana is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us for immediate removal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
