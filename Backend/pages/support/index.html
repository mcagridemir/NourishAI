Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a meal?

Tap the + button on the Dashboard or use the quick-add button in the Today view. You can search foods by name, scan a barcode, or describe your meal to the AI coach and it will estimate the nutrition for you.

How does the AI coach work?

Sana uses Anthropic's Claude AI to answer nutrition questions, analyse your meals, and provide personalised advice based on your goals. Your messages are sent over an encrypted connection. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Can I connect Apple Health?

Yes. Go to Settings → Integrations → Apple Health and grant the permissions you want. Sana can read steps, active energy, heart rate, and sleep, and write nutrition data back to Health.

Does Sana sync across my devices?

Yes, if you are signed into iCloud. Your food logs and settings sync privately through Apple's CloudKit — no Sana server ever sees your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Sana, and tap Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the end of the current billing period. You can also manage this in the App Store app.

I forgot my password / can't sign in.

Email passwords in Sana are stored locally on your device only — there is no server-side reset. If you used Sign in with Apple or Google, tap the corresponding button on the sign-in screen to authenticate via that provider instead. If you are locked out entirely, deleting and reinstalling the app will let you create a new account.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Sana and delete the app data. For further requests, email us.

The app is crashing or something isn't working.

Email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. Crash reports are sent automatically via Firebase Crashlytics (anonymously), which helps us fix issues quickly — but a direct report helps us prioritise.
