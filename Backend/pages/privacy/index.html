Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. Sana is designed to keep your health data on your device wherever possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

Health & Nutrition Data

  • Food logs, meal entries, and nutrition estimates you create in the app
  • Body metrics (weight, height, age, activity level) you enter manually
  • Fasting timers and hydration logs
  • Progress photos stored locally on your device — never uploaded to any server
  • HealthKit data you explicitly grant access to (steps, active energy, heart rate, sleep, etc.)

Account Data

  • Email address and encrypted password hash (stored locally in Keychain only — never sent to our servers)
  • Apple ID or Google account identifier when you sign in with those providers

Usage Data

  • Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Anonymous analytics events (screen views, feature usage) via Firebase Analytics — no personally identifiable information is included

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-powered nutrition analysis and coaching responses via the Claude API (Anthropic)
  • To sync your data across your own devices via iCloud / CloudKit
  • To improve app stability and performance (crash reports)
  • To understand which features are most useful (anonymous analytics)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. AI Processing

When you ask Sana's AI coach a question, the relevant context (your recent meals, goals, and any text you type) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API over an encrypted connection. This data is processed to generate your response and is not used to train Anthropic's models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.

Requests are proxied through our Cloudflare Worker, which rate-limits usage and does not log request bodies.

4. HealthKit

Sana requests read/write access to Apple HealthKit only for the categories you explicitly permit. HealthKit data is stored on-device and in your personal iCloud account. It is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Sana.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • On-device: All food logs, meals, and health data are stored locally using SwiftData and encrypted by iOS.
  • iCloud / CloudKit: If you are signed into iCloud, your data syncs privately through Apple's CloudKit. Apple cannot read this data.
  • Keychain: Credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain and are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Data Retention

Your data lives on your device and in your iCloud account. Deleting the app removes local data. You can delete your iCloud data at any time in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

7. Children's Privacy

Sana is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because most of your data lives on your device, you can delete it directly. For any requests or questions, contact us at the address below.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI responses
  • Google Firebase — Analytics & Crash reporting
  • Cloudflare — API proxy & rate limiting
  • Apple (HealthKit, CloudKit, StoreKit)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. Continued use of Sana after changes constitutes acceptance.
